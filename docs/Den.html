"This page will be a feature we hope to implement very soon, and is of high priority."

The den page is where a user's pets, familiars, and such is housed. It is where a user can do their daily interactions with their pet [aside from questing], such as incubating [egg stage pets], hatching [egg stage pets], cuddling [baby stage pets], petting, bathing, feeding, watering, playing, and dressing up.

A user would also, ideally, be able to see their pet's level, age, stats, and other fun info as well.
