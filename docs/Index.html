Welcome to Vakaroria Virtual Pets!

A petsite simulation game where you can design, play with, collect, raise, breed, train, dress up, share, quest and more with your very own pixel pets!
NOTE: We are not yet open for registration, though we hope to be by Spring 2026!

Above is a Fenrix, the founding species of VVP!

Online Play

Connect and interact with other players via our guild and forum boards features to fulfill all your socialization desires!

Safe and Welcoming Community

We strive to give our players a safe, welcoming, fun, and unique place to spend their time with their favorite pixel pets!

Free To Play

Vakaroria Virtual Pets is a free to play pet simulation site game, however we do offer on site purchases for those who may be interested. Anything available to the public on the site can be obtained with out spending real world money.
